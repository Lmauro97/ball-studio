Rolling Escape

Privacy Policy Last updated: June 24, 2026

Overview

Ball Studios ("we", "our") operates the mobile application Rolling Escape. This document describes how data is collected and used when you play the game.

Data we collect

Rolling Escape does not collect personal information through its own systems. All game progress — score, coins, owned skins, checkpoint level — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-party advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Interaction data with displayed ads
  • App usage information

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Privacy → Ads. Google's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal information and we will delete it promptly.

Data deletion

Local game data can be removed at any time by uninstalling the App or using the Reset Progress option in the game settings.
