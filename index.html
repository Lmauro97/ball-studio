Habits (Hábitos)

Privacy Policy Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

Ball Studios ("we", "our") operates the mobile application Habits (known as Hábitos in some markets, package ID: com.ballstudio.habitos.app). This document describes how data is collected and used when you use the App. Habits is a habit tracker and personal productivity tool that also offers an AI coaching feature and an optional cloud backup via Firebase.

Data we collect

Hábitos is built local-first: your habits, goals, diary entries, mood logs, audio voice memos, and points are stored on your device and never transmitted to our servers unless you choose to sign in. When you use the optional Google sign-in, the following additional data may be collected:

  • Google account display name and email address (used only for account identification)
  • A Firebase user ID linked to your account
  • Habit and goal data synced to Firestore for backup and cross-device access

You can use the App fully without creating an account. Anonymous usage does not transmit any personal data to our servers.

AI coach

The App includes an AI coaching feature powered by DeepSeek. In compliance with the EU AI Act (in force August 2026), we inform you that this feature uses an artificial intelligence system to generate personalized habit advice. You are always interacting with an AI, not a human coach.

When you use the AI coach, a summary of your habit and goal activity is sent to a secure backend proxy (hosted on Render) which forwards the request to DeepSeek (operated by DeepSeek AI, People's Republic of China). This summary contains aggregated habit statistics only — it does not include your name, email, diary entries, voice memos, or any directly identifying information. Requests are not stored by Ball Studios beyond the session duration.

International transfer notice: DeepSeek is based in China, a country without an EU adequacy decision. By using the AI coach, you acknowledge that your anonymized habit statistics will be transferred to and processed in China under DeepSeek's terms. If you do not wish this transfer, do not use the AI coach feature — all core habit-tracking functionality remains available without it.

DeepSeek's data practices are governed by DeepSeek's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display rewarded ads. Watching a rewarded ad is optional and unlocks additional AI coach interactions. AdMob may collect data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Interaction data with displayed ads
  • App usage information

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Privacy → Ads. Google's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

In-app purchases

The App offers an optional premium subscription processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store payment card details. All transactions are governed by Google Play's Terms of Service.

Push notifications

With your permission, the App sends local push notifications as habit reminders and weekly review prompts. These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not involve transmitting personal data to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data deletion

Local data can be removed at any time by uninstalling the App. If you signed in with a Google account, you can request deletion of your Firebase data by contacting us at the address below and we will process the request within 30 days. You can also export and delete your data using the backup and restore feature inside the App's profile screen.

Lawful basis (EEA & UK)

Where we process personal data, the lawful basis under GDPR is:

  • Contract — account data (name, email, Firebase ID) required to provide cloud sync and the signed-in service
  • Consent — personalized advertising via AdMob (EEA/UK); AI coach data transfer to DeepSeek
  • Legitimate interest — non-personalized ads; anonymous usage analytics to improve the App

Anonymous (non-signed-in) usage involves no personal data processing by Ball Studios.

Your rights

If you are in the EEA, UK, or California, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — receive your habit and goal data in a structured format (available via the App's export feature)
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw AI coach or ad consent at any time without affecting other features
  • Opt-out of data sharing (California) — opt out of ad data sharing via device settings
